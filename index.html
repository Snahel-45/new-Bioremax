About Bioremax Innovation

Bioremax Innovation Private Limited (BIPL) is a research-driven startup registered under I-Startup Rajasthan and MSE with the Rajasthan Government. Founded on 18th July 2024, BIPL specializes in creating innovative and environmentally sustainable technologies. Based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, we are powered by a dedicated team of scientists and engineers striving for ecological impact.

Our cutting-edge enzyme-based bioremediation process effectively breaks down oily sludge, crude residues, and drill cuttings. This method is 100% natural, eco-friendly, and cost-effective—transforming harmful hydrocarbons into safe, non-toxic fatty acids using enzymes from naturally occurring microbes.

As the first Rajasthan-recognized science-based environmental restoration startup, BIPL leads in biotechnological innovation with its proprietary super enzyme—a revolutionary solution for oil pollutant treatment and sustainable site restoration.
